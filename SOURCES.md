## Access Notes

- **Congressional documents:** All .gov websites. Free access. No registration.
- **Patent documents:** Google Patents. Free access. No registration.
- **FOIA documents (Archive.org):** Free access. No registration.
- **DocumentCloud PDFs:** Use `s3.documentcloud.org/documents/{ID}/{filename}` for direct access (bypasses Cloudflare).
- **Chinese sources:** May require VPN from some regions. Screenshots available locally.
- **GISAID data:** Requires GISAID account and acceptance of data sharing terms. Metadata removed from Bloom's GitHub history per GISAID compliance.
