## Proven vs Unproven

### Proven
- Zhou Yusen was a senior PLA researcher at AMMS (State Key Lab of Pathogen and Biosecurity)
- Zhou filed patent CN111333704A on 24 Feb 2020 with mouse immunogenicity data
- The patent uses RBD-Fc technology — the same platform from Zhou's MERS work (2013-2019)
- Zhou and Shi Zhengli (WIV) were co-authors on a MERS ADE paper submitted 1 Oct 2019
- Chen Wei (AMMS) led a military team to Wuhan on 26 Jan 2020
- AMMS/CanSino vaccine patent granted August 2020

### Unproven
- That Zhou's vaccine existed before December 2019
- How Zhou Yusen died (exact date, cause, location)
- Whether AMMS and WIV collaborated on gain-of-function specifically
- Whether Zhou's work received NIH funding
- Whether Zhou was married to Lanying Du (co-authorship confirmed; marriage not)

## Open Questions (Dossier 3)

1. **What is the exact date and cause of Zhou Yusen's death?** — No primary source found. Chinese forums claim he fell from a WIV roof in May 2020 (unverified).
2. **Did AMMS and WIV directly collaborate on coronavirus research beyond the 2019 MERS ADE paper?** — No co-authorship papers found with both Zhou and WIV-affiliated researchers beyond the JVI paper.
3. **Did Zhou's team receive NIH funding?** — Multiple media sources claim "three NIH grants" — no NIH RePORTER entry found under Zhou Yusen's name.
4. **Was Zhou married to Lanying Du?** — Co-authorship established. Yuri Deigin (Medium) explicitly states this. No marriage certificate found.
5. **Is Zhou's patent the earliest COVID-19 vaccine patent in the world?** — ProPublica corrected the claim. Comparative patent date analysis needed.
