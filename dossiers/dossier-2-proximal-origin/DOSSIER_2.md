## Proven vs Unproven

### Proven
- The Proximal Origin paper stated SARS-CoV-2 is "not a laboratory construct"
- FOIA emails document Fauci/Collins coordination to "push back" on lab-leak
- Collins emailed Fauci: "This is what we need to push back on"
- Edward Holmes later walked back the paper's strong language
- The paper was used as a messaging tool by NIH leadership
- The paper was Correspondence format (not full peer-reviewed research article)

### Unproven
- Whether the coordination constitutes "narrative suppression" or "normal science communication"
- Whether non-public data influenced the paper's conclusions
- Whether there was deliberate suppression of alternative hypotheses
