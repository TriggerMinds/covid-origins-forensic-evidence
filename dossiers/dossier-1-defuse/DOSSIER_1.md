## Proven vs Unproven

### Proven
- DEFUSE proposal planned furin cleavage site insertion
- Baric had published chimeric coronavirus work with furin sites pre-2019
- SARS-CoV-2 contains a PRRA furin cleavage site
- Baric admitted the furin idea was his and he "forgot" to disclose DEFUSE
- Baric and Li proposed inserting furin into RaTG13 in early 2020
- EcoHealth worked with WIV/Shi Zhengli on bat coronavirus surveillance

### Unproven
- That DEFUSE-funded work directly produced SARS-CoV-2
- That the furin cleavage site is definitively synthetic
- That the exact technique proposed in DEFUSE was used to create SARS-CoV-2
