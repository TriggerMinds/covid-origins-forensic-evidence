## About This Repository

This repository contains a structured forensic investigation into the origins of SARS-CoV-2, organized as three independent evidence dossiers plus supporting evidence chains, source files, and methodology documentation. Every claim is sourced. Primary sources are prioritized. Secondary and media sources are clearly labeled.

**What this repository is:**
- A systematic collection of publicly verifiable primary and secondary sources
- Three independently constructed evidence dossiers
- Explicit evidence chains with claim-to-source mapping
- Transparent documentation of what is proven, plausible, and unproven

**What this repository is not:**
- A political argument
- A theory confirmation exercise
- A complete answer to COVID-19 origins
- Legal or criminal analysis

## Evidence Chains

### PRJNA612766 — Deleted Early Wuhan Sequences
**Status:** COMPLETE (6/6 gaps resolved)

Jesse Bloom recovered 13 partial SARS-CoV-2 sequences from deleted SRA BioProject PRJNA612766 (Wang et al. 2020, Renmin Hospital/Wuhan University). The progenitor of SARS-CoV-2 carries 3 mutations toward bat coronaviruses (C8782T, T28144C, C29095T) relative to Wuhan-Hu-1. Wuhan University deleted two SRA projects; only one was published.

## What Is Proven

1. DEFUSE proposal (2018) planned furin cleavage site insertion into bat SARS-like coronaviruses
2. Ralph Baric admitted the idea "was clearly mine" and "forgot" to mention DEFUSE in early briefings
3. Fauci/Collins FOIA emails document coordination to "push back" on lab-leak theory
4. Zhou Yusen (AMMS/PLA) filed patent CN111333704A on Feb 24, 2020 with mouse data
5. AMMS-WIV collaborated on MERS ADE research in 2019 (paper submitted Oct 1, 2019)
6. PRJNA612766 contained early Wuhan sequences deleted from SRA; recovered by Bloom via Google Cloud
7. The COVID-19 progenitor is 3 mutations closer to bat coronaviruses than Huanan Market viruses
8. **NEW:** WHO SAGO (June 2025) states all hypotheses remain viable, citing missing data from China
9. **NEW:** Multiple SRA datasets foundational to natural-origin hypotheses were revised/altered (USRTK)
10. **NEW:** PRJNA612766 is not an isolated deletion—a broader pattern exists across COVID-origin datasets

## What Is NOT Proven

1. That SARS-CoV-2 was exactly created in a laboratory
2. That Zhou's vaccine existed before December 2019
3. How Zhou Yusen died (no death certificate)
4. That military preparedness indicated foreknowledge of the outbreak
5. That the furin cleavage site is definitively synthetic (it can occur naturally)
